Meatball Sub Casserole

Why Meatball Sub Casserole?

When I was a kid one of my staple sandwhiches at Subway was a meatball sub. The way the meatballs blended with the tomato sauce and the cheese made for a super flavorful and delicous sandwhich. I had to find a fun and creative way to bring this sandwhich into a delicious meal at home and alas I found Meatball Sub Casserole. Please follow the instructions below and once complete I am very confident you will be having more than one piece!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen garlic bread
  • 2 (24 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 40 italian-style frozen meatballs
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Heat garlic bread spread side up on a baking sheet in the oven until golden brown around 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F.
  4. Bring spaghetti sauce to a boil in a large pot. Place frozen meatballs in the sauce. Reduce heat to low and simmer until meatballs are heated through which takes about 20 minutes.
  5. Tear garlic bread into bite-size pieces and spread into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour spaghetti sauce and meatballs over garlic bread, coating bread with sauce and distributing meatballs evenly. Spread mozzarella cheese over top in a layer, followed by a layer of Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake casserole until cheese has melted which should take around 20 to 25 minutes.

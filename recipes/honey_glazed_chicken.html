Honey Glazed Chicken

Why Honey Glazed Chicken?

When I was growing up there was a local grocery store that had chinese available in it and I would go there frequently for one dish. That dish would be honey glazed chicken. It had a great balance of the sweet honey mixed with the red pepper flakes which made the dish a flavor explosion in my mouth. So I had to make a version at home and after much trial and error I found a recipe that hit just right to give me that same sweet and spicy sensation. Please follow the instructions listed below and I hope you enjoy this delicous meal!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces

Directions

  1. Whisk together soy sauce, red pepper flakes, and honey in a bowl then set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in the oil until lightly brown which should take about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir chicken until the center is no longer pink and the sauce has thickened which should take about 5 minutes.

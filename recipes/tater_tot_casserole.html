Tater Tot Casserole

Why Tater Tot Casserole?

When going out to a restaurant and I have the choice of fries or tater tots I choose tater tots. Their short cylindrical shape fried to crispy perfection is just to much to pass up. So I started to wonder if there was a dish I could make at home that revolved around tater tots and I came upon tater tot casserole. The great thing about this dish is that it is simple to make and it is only 6 ingredients. So without further ado please following the instructions below so you can create the great dish known as tater tot casserole.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen tater tots
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
